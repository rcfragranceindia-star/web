About the company

Rangreza Creations, the house behind RC Fragnance.

RC Fragnance is the fragrance division of Rangreza Creations — a house built around a simple belief: scent should be designed, not defaulted to. We compose essential oil blends and engineer diffusion systems that work together as one considered experience, not two separate purchases.

Every blend is developed for diffusion first, every system is chosen for consistency, and every space we work with — homes, offices, retail floors, spas and hospitality venues — gets a scent profile that fits its size, its mood, and the impression it wants to leave.

Our Story

Rooted in craft, built for scale.

“Rangreza” is the old word for a dyer — someone who colours cloth by hand, thread by thread. Rangreza Creations borrows that same idea for scent: a space isn’t finished until it has a mood, and mood is something you compose deliberately, not something that happens by default.

RC Fragnance is where that philosophy becomes a product line. We don’t treat oil and hardware as separate purchases — a blend is only as good as the diffuser carrying it into the room, so both are developed together, out of Hyderabad, for homes, offices, retail floors, spas and hospitality venues.

01

Source

Raw materials are selected for scent quality first — traditional florals like mogra and jasmine sambac alongside citrus, fruit and woody accords.

02

Blend

Every formula is composed in top, middle and base layers, so the scent develops over time in a room instead of reading as one flat note.

03

Test

Blends are run through our own diffusion hardware before release, since a formula that smells right in a bottle can still perform poorly in the air.

04

Deliver

Oil and hardware are matched to the space — its size, its airflow, its mood — rather than sold as a generic, one-size-fits-all kit.
